The marketplace enables the P2P trading of NFTs with royalties and fallback fees, by utilising the allowance/approval API. When you create a listing the NFT will remain in your account and is put on sale which allows other users to buy the NFT. When you purchase an NFT from another user the NFT is sent to your account and the payment is sent to the seller’s account (minus any royalty fees from the creator and marketplace transaction fees).
